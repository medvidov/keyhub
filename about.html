SIMPLIFY THE HOBBY


KEYHUB'S MISSION


For anyone who's fallen into the rabbit hole called custom mechanical keyboards, it's no secret that getting your hands on parts are extremely difficult. From limited supplies to sometimes even raffles, many variables make this hobby hard to enjoy. KeyHub aims to help hobbyists overcome at least one of those obstacles, primarily by erasing the borders drawn by various vendors. The number of group buys hosted for different parts on different vendors makes it impossible for the casual hobbyist to easily assess all the choices. Links to specific vendors and group buys, which are usually only found on forums, are lost in a flood of posts; it's hard to keep track of what's available and for how long.
